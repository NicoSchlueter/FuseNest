This app automatically places parts on a sheet in a material-efficient manner given sheet size & spacing using an open-source nesting algorithm.

Features:
 - Automatically lays out parts
 - Starts a new sheet once the first one is full.
 - Adjustable spacing between parts
 - Continues to look for better solutions after the first one is found.

This is the professional version, a free version is also available for non-commercial, educational, and trial use. Users of the professional version are prioritized regarding support and bug reports.

This is the free version for non-commercial, educational, and trial use only. A professional version for commercial use, including priority support is also available.
